PRIVACY POLICY — TripTuner

Last Updated: [December 10, 2025]

TripTuner (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides a community-driven platform for discovering and sharing local travel itineraries. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the TripTuner mobile application (“App”).

By using TripTuner, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including:

Email address (used for account creation and login via Firebase Authentication)

Content you submit, including itineraries, descriptions, photos, comments

Profile information, such as your display name or profile photo (if added)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

Device information (model, OS version)

Usage data (interactions within the app)

Crash logs and diagnostics

General location information (city/region) — not precise GPS

1.3 Firebase Services

TripTuner uses:

Firebase Authentication

Firebase Firestore Database

Firebase Cloud Storage

Firebase Analytics (if enabled)

These services may collect:

IP address

Authentication tokens

Anonymous usage statistics

All Firebase data is stored and secured by Google according to their privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Provide core features of the App

Allow you to create an account and log in

Save, store, and display itineraries you share

Improve the App’s design and functionality

Maintain security and prevent abuse

Respond to support inquiries

Conduct analytics and usage insights (if enabled)

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. User-Generated Content

TripTuner allows users to submit and share itineraries.
You understand and agree that:

Content you submit may be visible to other users

You may delete your content at any time

We may remove content that violates our guidelines

You must not submit illegal, copyrighted, or harmful content

4. Cookies and Tracking

TripTuner does not use cookies.
Firebase may use identifiers to support analytics and crash reporting.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information only with trusted service providers, including:

Firebase (Google LLC) for authentication, database storage, hosting

Apple App Store for analytics and crash reports

We do not share your data with advertisers or sell your information.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

Encrypted communication (HTTPS/TLS)

Secure authentication

Access controls and permissions

However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Access or delete your data

Correct inaccurate information

Request a copy of your data

Withdraw your consent (by deleting your account)

You may submit these requests via the Support Email below.

8. Children’s Privacy

TripTuner is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.
Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
